Our Concept

Pink Pedals, where cycling is a habit, is a bold initiative towards society in which we are working to provide all age groups a better idea of how to be fit, healthy and happy with minimum efforts. Pink Pedals is for everyone, men, women, and children.

Pink Pedals in a unique and new concept to promote cycling, we provide good quality, high end geared and Non-geared cycles on rent. We are specialised in organising corporate cycling events, Cyclothons, Customised Cycling city tours.

We also work with corporates to ensure wellness and healthy living of employee’s. We promote Cycle with Work with our hashtag #CycletoWork #CycleWithWork

Our Vision

Our Vision is our commitment towards betterment of our environment, our people.

We aim at promoting Sustainable mode of Mobility and prioritising old, but yet a new way of commutation and leisure i.e. CYCLES

Founder

The mind behind the concept is Ms Pooja Vijay who is a Commerce Graduate from reputed educational institute Kanoria Girls College. She also completed her Law from Rajasthan University.

She worked by giants MNCs like Genpact and Infosys earlier.

Pooja Vijay, an young entrepreneur and social activist who founded Pink Pedals in 2017, the Rajasthan’s first and largest cycle rental and bike sharing services with the vision of promoting bicycles. She was also chosen as first “Bicycle Mayor of Jaipur” by Amsterdam based organisation BYCS working on a mission of 50by30 means 50 per cent of city rides on cycle by 2030.

OUR
SERVICES

  • Cycle Rentals
  • Public Bicycle Sharing
  • Corporate Mobility Solutions
  • Corporate Cycling Events
  • Cyclothons
  • Customised City Cycle Tours

Pink Pedals provides best in class fully maintained hi-end international brand Geared and Non-Geared cycles at a very reasonable price for any duration, an hour or a day, a week or a month. Keeping the easiness and safety of rider in concern, we provide all necessary accessories and gears complimentary with Cycles. It’s easy and safe always hiring a bicycle from Pink Pedals. We do have a big fleet of cycles for all occasions and need. We have attractive cycles like Tandem cycles (2-Seater) or Fat tyre bicycles. Our fleet is our USP which covers everyone, be it men, women or kids. Our tariff slides per hire duration or quantity. The more you ride, the less you pay.

Pink Pedals have a hand on experience in Public Bicycle sharing or end point connectivity cycle services. Currently Pink Pedals PBS is operational at Mahindra World City Jaipur, India’s biggest integrated society with a big fleet of cycles on land. We support “Cycle to Work” and “Cycle with Work”. (Get more knowledge about current operational PBS, go to https://www.mahindraworldcity.com/events_at_mwc/mahindra-world-city-jaipur-launches-eco-friendly-bicycle-sharing-services/ )

Pink Pedals, from the very first day is working towards providing Sustainable mobility solutions to corporates whether it’s about employee commutation, or employee well-being or some event. Customisation in the services and process is done per client’s need or requirement. (Ref. for the same can be provided on request)

Pink Pedals, one of the major area of working is Cycling events or large scale group rides. We have marked more than 200 corporate and government cycling events and still adding. We are masters in organising such events, provides all arrangement from Cycles to T-Shirts, Medals to Certificate, Snacks to Music, Gifts to branding, route map to maintenance. You have to just come, ride and enjoy, Rest Pink Pedals will take care.

Large scale cyclothons are being organised in every short span of time to keep up the spirit of people on. Like Juniors Cyclothon, Saksham Cycle rally, Independence day ride, Night ride are to name a few. We are specialised in organising Cyclothons and provides all arrangement from Cycles to T-Shirts, Medals to Certificate, Snacks to Music, route map to maintenance. You have to just come, ride and enjoy, Rest Pink Pedals will take care.

Are you looking for something specially designed for you as per your holiday plans, We caters customised city cycling tours for Solo riders, corporate guests and tourist, to have sneak peak of every nook and corner of Pinkcity. The tour may be self-guided or guided; we provide complete guidance and support to make your travel worthy.
