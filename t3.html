PINK Legacy

The kings own country, view the Amer Fort closely being on saddle and impressive nearby places.


RIDE LEVEL - Normal 20 KMs ride


AREAS COVERED - Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal. Amer Fort, Sagar Lake, Close look of Aravalli Hills, Durga Mandir (Temple), Jagat Shiromani Mandir (Temple)


Meeting Point- Hawa Mahal (Wind Palace)


ITINARY

1. Assembling & Introduction at Hawa Mahal, briefing about the ride, safety points and cycle gears.
2.Feel the cool breeze at small stop over Jal Mahal (water palace) and relax to gear up for the fascinating journey of king’s era.
3. As you pedal, you will reach to the landmark of Jaipur heritage, The Amer fort. Enjoy the outer side of fort and move forward to see some amazing places like, Sagar Lake, Panna Heera ka kund (Step Well) etc. Enjoy packed
4.Will move forward to our starting point to conclude our Pink Legacy with breakfast at local’s house to witness real Pink city or at some famous stop. (Depend on Availability)
5. Ending of Pink Legacy ride

2. Mobile Pouch.
3. Mineral water bottles.
4. Breakfast and tea/coffee/juice with local food tasting.
5. Multi-lingual Guide and an Escort (for taking care of riders) .
6. Lock and key.
7. Backup vehicle/cycles.
